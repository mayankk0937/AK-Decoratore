The Owner

Meet Mr. Amit Kumar

Owner & Factory Director

AK Decoratore is run by Mr. Amit Kumar, who believes in delivering premium-grade drapery and custom window layouts directly from our manufacturing factory in Anand Parbat, Karol Bagh. By cutting out middle-men and costly retail showroom markups, we pass the savings directly to you.

With over 25 years of hands-on industry expertise, Mr. Amit Kumar personally supervises our design consultations and quality inspections. From selecting the finest linen and blackout fabrics to ensuring laser-precise site measurements, his obsession with precision ensures a flawless finish for every home and office space.

"Humari apni factory hai, isliye hum har ek drape aur installation ko mathematical precision ke sath custom-craft karte hain. Quality se koi samjhauta nahi."
— Mr. Amit Kumar, Owner

In-House Manufacturing

Our Karol Bagh factory handles stitching, cutting, and assembly. This gives us complete control over stitching density, lining quality, and fast delivery timelines.

Master Drapers & Tailors

Our experienced factory tailors meticulously handcraft double-pleated champagne sheers, heavy blackout panels, and modern roller fabric assemblies to exact window specifications.

Expert Installation Crew

Our white-glove setup team mounts channels, luxury brass rods, and smart motorized tracks seamlessly, ensuring a tidy, hassle-free installation at your site.
