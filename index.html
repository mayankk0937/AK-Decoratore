Our Seamless Process

How We Dress Your Spaces

From measuring massive glass doors to fitting commercial office blinds, we manage the entire process to secure absolute design and functional perfection.

01

Fabric & Design Selection

Choose from our premium catalogs of imported linen, luxury cottons, blockout materials, sheer fabrics, and modern blind systems. We match colors and textures perfectly to your interior vibe.

02

Free Expert Home Visit

No need to travel. Our professional consultants will visit your home or site directly, bringing catalog physical hangers and samples so you can touch the fabrics in your space's natural light.

03

Laser-Precise Measurement

A perfect drape starts with perfect numbers. We use high-precision laser equipment to calibrate width, height, structural window clearances, and stack-backs to the exact millimeter.

04

In-House Factory Manufacturing

By manufacturing directly in our own facilities, we avoid expensive middle-men markups and secure rigid quality checks. Experienced craftsmen stitch curtains with deep double pleats, sturdy eyelets, or premium header tapes.

05

Flawless White-Glove Installation

Our skilled installations team mounts premium tracks, silent motor channels, and classic rods safely, then carefully drapes, steams, and trains your new curtains for that crisp, high-end editorial ripple effect.
